## 🔬 Core Research Focus

Our research is centered at the intersection of mechanical engineering, laser physics, and physical chemistry. We focus on developing advanced optical diagnostics to study high-temperature gas flows, combustion chemistry, and fast transient phenomena.

### ⚡ Laser Absorption Spectroscopy & Sensors
* **High-Precision Diagnostics:** Development of rapid-tuning, high-accuracy laser sensors for non-intrusive measurements in reacting and non-reacting flows.
* **Extreme Conditions:** Quantitative sensing of temperature, pressure, and multi-species concentrations in harsh environments (e.g., engines, shock tubes, and high-speed wind tunnels).

### 💥 Shock Wave Physics, Chemistry & Kinetics
* **Reaction Kinetics:** Utilizing state-of-the-art shock tubes to investigate fundamental gas-phase chemical reaction pathways and rate parameters.
* **Fuel Modeling:** Determining crucial chemical kinetics data to validate and refine combustion models for conventional, alternative, and sustainable fuels.

### 🚀 Advanced Propulsion & Energy Science
* **Propulsion Systems:** Studying the fundamental science behind gas turbines, scramjets, detonative propulsion, and next-generation propulsion concepts.
* **Clean Energy:** Investigating clean energy carriers, low-carbon fuels, and carbon mitigation technologies to address global energy challenges.

## 💻 Open-Source & Computational Tools

We develop and maintain specialized code, libraries, and utilities to support the broader research community in gas dynamics, spectroscopy, and chemical kinetics:

* **📡 Laser & Camera SDKs:** Driver interfaces and processing tools for high-speed ultraviolet (UV), visible, and infrared imaging systems (e.g., VUV Camera SDKs).
* **📐 Spectroscopy Modeling:** Computational tools for simulating and analyzing molecular absorption spectra at elevated temperatures and pressures (e.g., Universal Spectra Model).
* **🛠️ Lab Automation:** Python and MATLAB utilities for real-time laser control, high-frequency data acquisition, and automated laboratory backups.
